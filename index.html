About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student double-majoring in Economics and International Studies: International Business at UC San Diego. I plan to enroll in the Bachelors/Masters program at the School of Global Policy and Strategy here at UC San Diego in order to earn my Masters in International Affairs by 2021.

During my free time, I enjoy going to concerts, spending time at the beach or spending time at Sunset Cliffs.

Projects


CARE Center Internship

This year, I started an intership for the San Diego County District Attorney's office as a mentor for underserved and under-represented youth in Southeast San Diego.

Students ranging from elementary school through college come to the Center in order to receive personal or academic assistance from University mentors.

Each One, Reach One

I have been a mentor for Each One, Reach One in Thurgood Marshall College for one year.

Through one-on-one meetings and group events, the mentors in Each One, Reach One spend time and provide academic and personal assistance to incoming freshman seeking guidance.
